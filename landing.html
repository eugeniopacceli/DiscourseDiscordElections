Discourse Discord

Discourse Discord provides a comfortable community to those all over the world who want a casual place to talk about politics.

We host a private chat room on Discord, with text and voice channels, primarily in english.

A foundational principle of Discourse Discord is that power should lie within the hands of the people. Active and contributional users here will gain the privilege to vote on who they want to be a mod, or even run for a moderation position themselves!

You are visiting the root of our web system.

In this machine we host the AutoAuxiliary bot and the current voting system.

Stick around and read the rules!

This community is self-administered and is not officially associated with Discord, the company behind the Discord platform.
